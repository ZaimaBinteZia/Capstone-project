The Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is one of the most iconic landmarks in the world, renowned for its immense scale and historical significance. It is a series of fortifications made of stone, brick, tamped earth, wood, and other materials, stretching over 13,000 miles across northern China. The wall was constructed to protect Chinese states and empires from invasions and raids by nomadic groups from the north.
